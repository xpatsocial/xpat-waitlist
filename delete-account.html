Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Here's how to delete your x/pat account and all associated data.

Option 1: In the App

  1. Open the x/pat app
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Tap Settings
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion

Option 2: By Email

Send an email to alex@xpat.social from the email address associated with your account, and we'll process your deletion request.

7-day grace period: After requesting deletion, your account will be deactivated immediately but not permanently removed for 7 days. During this period you can sign back in to cancel the deletion. After 7 days, all your data will be permanently and irreversibly deleted.
